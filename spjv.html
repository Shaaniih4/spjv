About Us

Our History & Vision

Sutarpara jatiya Vidyalaya was established in 2004 with a vision to provide quality education that balances academic excellence with character development. Over the decades, we have grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to succeed in a rapidly changing world. We believe in fostering curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking in a safe and inclusive environment.

With a student-teacher ratio of 15:1, we ensure personalized attention to each child. Our campus spans over 1 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including science labs, libraries, sports complexes, and art studios.

Academics

Language Arts

We offer comprehensive language programs in English, Spanish, French, and Mandarin. Our focus is on communication skills, creative writing, and literary analysis.

Arts & Humanities

Our arts curriculum includes visual arts, music, theater, and dance. Students have opportunities to participate in exhibitions, concerts, and theatrical productions.

Physical Education

We promote physical fitness and sportsmanship through a variety of athletic programs, soccer, and yoga.
